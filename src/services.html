Services

Our Services

We offer tailored consulting solutions designed to help businesses grow, adapt, and succeed. Explore how we can support your goals.

Market Research & Feasibility Studies

We provide in-depth market and sector research based on real data from the field. Our studies help clients understand demand, competition, pricing, and customer behavior. We evaluate project feasibility by combining market analysis with clear financial assumptions, giving investors a solid foundation to assess risks and opportunities before making decisions.

Financial Modeling & Business Planning

We build tailored financial models and business plans to help investors and project owners plan with confidence. Our models include detailed forecasts for revenue, costs, profit margins, and cash flows. We also provide profitability analysis using key metrics such as IRR, payback period, ROI, and break-even point. All based on realistic and transparent assumptions.

Valuation Services

We provide clear, independent valuations for land, real estate, businesses, and other assets. Our approach is based on market data, financial performance, and project-specific factors. We apply recognised valuation methods including Discounted Cash Flow (DCF) analysis, comparable company analysis, and precedent transactions. Whether for acquisition, investment, or internal planning, we help clients understand the true value of what they own or plan to buy, with transparent and objective analysis they can trust.

Investment Advisory & Local Support

We guide investors through the early stages of entering the Libyan and Tunisian markets. Our support includes identifying promising opportunities, structuring investment plans, and building financial projections. We also help clients understand and navigate local regulations, connect with reliable partners, and take the right steps to move their projects forward smoothly and efficiently.
